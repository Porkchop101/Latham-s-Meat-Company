What We're About!

Here we tell you the history about Latham's Meat Company and how it became what it is today.

Paul Latham

Meet Paul Latham, founder and proprietor of Latham's Meat Company. Paul and his brother learned how to smoke a whole hog from their father, Travis Latham. He used these family traditions to open a resturant in Jackson, Tennessee. This thriving business has become a busy country resturant and butcher shop that serves hundreds of people per week. His motto has always been: "Give people plenty to eat and they will come back."

We are open Monday thru Saturday, 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

And we are located at 3517 US-45, Jackson, Tennessee 38305.
